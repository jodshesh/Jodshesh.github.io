Boracay

Resort Island in the Western Visayas Region of the Philippines

Boracay is a small island in the central Philippines. It's known for its resorts and beaches. Along the west coast, Beach is backed by palm trees, bars and restaurants.

Bohol

Central Visayas Region

Bohol is known for coral reefs and unusual geological formations, notably the Chocolate Hills. On the main island, near the town of Carmen, these 1,200 or so symmetrical mounds turn cocoa-brown in the dry season, contrasting with the surrounding jungle's greenery.
